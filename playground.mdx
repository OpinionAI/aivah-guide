## Playground Overview

The Aivah Playground provides a safe, experimental environment where you can test agent configurations, try different prompts, and refine your AI avatars before deploying them to production.

<img src="/images/playground.png" alt="Aivah Playground Interface" />

## Getting Started

<Steps>
  <Step title="Access Playground">
    Navigate to the Playground section from your dashboard
  </Step>
  <Step title="Select or Create Agent">
    Choose an existing agent or create a new one for testing
  </Step>
  <Step title="Configure Settings">
    Adjust parameters and settings in the configuration panel
  </Step>
  <Step title="Start Testing">
    Begin conversations and experiments with your agent
  </Step>
</Steps>
