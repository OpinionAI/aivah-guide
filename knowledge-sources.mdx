## Knowledge Sources Overview

Empower your AI agents with comprehensive knowledge bases by uploading, organizing, and managing various data sources that enhance their expertise and response accuracy.

<img src="/images/knowledge-sources.png" alt="Knowledge Sources Management" />

## Getting Started with Knowledge Sources

<Steps>
  <Step title="Access Knowledge Management">
    Navigate to the Knowledge Sources section in your agent dashboard
  </Step>
  <Step title="Upload Your Content">
    Add documents, websites, or data sources relevant to your use case
  </Step>
  <Step title="Organize and Structure">
    Categorize and tag your knowledge for optimal retrieval
  </Step>
  <Step title="Test and Validate">
    Verify that your agent can access and use the knowledge effectively
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Best Practices

### Knowledge Strategy
<Steps>
  <Step title="Define Scope">
    Clearly identify what knowledge your agents need to be effective
  </Step>
  <Step title="Prioritize Sources">
    Focus on high-value, frequently accessed information first
  </Step>
  <Step title="Establish Governance">
    Create clear policies for knowledge creation and maintenance
  </Step>
  <Step title="Monitor and Optimize">
    Regularly assess and improve knowledge effectiveness
  </Step>
</Steps>

### Content Management
- **Quality First**: Prioritize accuracy and relevance over quantity
- **Regular Updates**: Establish schedules for content review and refresh
- **User Feedback**: Actively collect and respond to user feedback
- **Performance Tracking**: Monitor how knowledge impacts agent effectiveness
