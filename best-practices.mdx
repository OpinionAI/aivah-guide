## Getting Started Right

Follow these best practices to maximize your success with Aivah AI avatars.

### Setting Up Your Agent

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Choose the Right Model">
    Select AI models based on your specific use case:
    - **GPT-4** for complex reasoning and detailed responses
    - **Claude** for creative and analytical tasks
    - **Gemini** for multimodal interactions
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Optimize Your Prompts">
    - Be specific about your agent's role and purpose
    - Include context about your industry or domain
    - Set clear boundaries and expectations
    - Use examples to guide behavior
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Configure Knowledge Sources">
    - Upload relevant documents and data
    - Keep information up-to-date
    - Organize content logically
    - Remove outdated information regularly
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

### Communication Best Practices

<CardGroup cols={2}>
  <Card title="Clear Instructions" icon="bullhorn">

### Performance Optimization

- **Monitor Usage**: Track your agent's performance and user engagement
- **A/B Testing**: Try different configurations to find what works best
- **Feedback Loop**: Collect and act on user feedback regularly
- **Resource Management**: Balance model complexity with response speed

### Security & Privacy

<Warning>
  Always review and sanitize any sensitive information before adding it to your agent's knowledge base.
</Warning>

- Implement proper access controls
- Regularly audit data and permissions
- Follow data retention policies
- Monitor for potential security issues

### Troubleshooting Common Issues

| Issue | Solution |
|-------|----------|
| Slow responses | Optimize model selection or reduce context length |
| Inconsistent answers | Improve prompt clarity and add more examples |
| Limited knowledge | Update knowledge sources and training data |
| User confusion | Enhance onboarding and provide clearer instructions |
