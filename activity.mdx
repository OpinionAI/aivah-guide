## Privacy and Compliance

<Warning>
  All activity monitoring respects user privacy and complies with data protection regulations.
</Warning>

### Data Protection
- **Anonymization Options**: Remove personal identifiers from monitoring data
- **Access Controls**: Restrict monitoring data access to authorized personnel
- **Retention Policies**: Configurable data retention and deletion schedules
- **Audit Trails**: Track who accesses monitoring data and when

### Compliance Features
- **GDPR Support**: Right to deletion and data portability
- **SOX Compliance**: Financial data integrity and controls
- **Industry Standards**: Healthcare, financial, and other sector requirements
- **Custom Policies**: Configure monitoring to meet specific compliance needs
