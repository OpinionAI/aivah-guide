## Getting Started

### Basic Interaction

1. **Select Your Agent**: Choose from your available AI avatars
2. **Choose Interaction Mode**: Pick between chat, voice, or mixed mode
3. **Start Conversing**: Begin your interaction naturally

### Switching Between Modes

<Steps>
  <Step title="Access Mode Selector">
    Click the mode toggle in the top navigation bar
  </Step>
  <Step title="Choose Your Preferred Mode">
    Select from chat, voice, or scene switching options
  </Step>
  <Step title="Confirm Selection">
    Your interface will update to reflect the new interaction mode
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Advanced Features

### Context Awareness

Your AI avatar maintains context throughout conversations:
- Remembers previous interactions
- Understands ongoing projects
- Builds on established relationships

### Personalization

The more you interact, the better your avatar understands:
- Your communication style
- Your preferences and interests
- Your work patterns and goals

<Tip>
  Regular interaction helps your AI avatar provide more personalized and relevant responses.
</Tip>

### Multi-Modal Capabilities

Combine different interaction modes for enhanced experiences:
- Switch between text and voice mid-conversation
- Use visual cues alongside verbal communication
- Integrate with external tools and services
