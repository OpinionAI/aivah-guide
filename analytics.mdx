## Privacy and Security

<Warning>
  All analytics comply with privacy regulations and maintain user anonymity where required.
</Warning>

### Data Protection
- **Anonymization**: Remove or mask personal identifiers
- **Aggregation**: Present data in aggregate form to protect individual privacy
- **Access Controls**: Restrict analytics access to authorized personnel
- **Retention Policies**: Implement appropriate data retention and deletion

### Compliance Standards
- **GDPR Compliance**: Right to deletion, data portability, and consent management
- **HIPAA Support**: Healthcare data protection and privacy requirements
- **SOX Compliance**: Financial data integrity and audit requirements
- **Industry Standards**: Sector-specific compliance and regulatory requirements
