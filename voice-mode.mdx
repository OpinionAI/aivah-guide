## Optimization Tips

### For Best Recognition
- **Speak Clearly**: Articulate words distinctly
- **Normal Pace**: Neither too fast nor too slow
- **Consistent Volume**: Maintain steady speaking volume
- **Reduce Background Noise**: Use in quiet environments when possible

### Hardware Recommendations
- **Good Microphone**: Invest in quality audio input
- **Headphones**: Reduce echo and feedback
- **Stable Connection**: Ensure reliable internet for real-time processing
- **Updated Browser**: Use latest version for best compatibility

## Privacy & Security

<Warning>
  Voice data is processed securely and can be configured for local processing only.
</Warning>

- **Data Encryption**: All voice data encrypted in transit
- **Local Processing**: Option for on-device speech recognition
- **No Permanent Storage**: Voice data not stored permanently
- **Privacy Controls**: Fine-grained privacy settings available

## Troubleshooting

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Microphone Not Working">
    - Check browser/app microphone permissions
    - Test microphone in system settings
    - Try refreshing the page or restarting app
    - Verify microphone hardware connectivity
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Poor Speech Recognition">
    - Reduce background noise
    - Speak more clearly and slowly
    - Check internet connection stability
    - Switch to a better microphone
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Audio Playback Issues">
    - Check system volume settings
    - Verify audio output device
    - Test with different browsers/apps
    - Clear browser cache and cookies
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Latency Problems">
    - Check internet connection speed
    - Close unnecessary applications
    - Switch to lower quality settings
    - Try edge servers closer to your location
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>
